# 🤖 CallBy AI

**CallBy AI** is a next-generation, AI-powered contact center solution that helps organizations **digitize their customer service operations**. Designed to provide seamless voice and text-based communication across multiple platforms, CallBy AI enhances both the **efficiency of support teams** and **customer satisfaction**.

---

## 🌍 What We Do

CallBy AI offers an **intelligent, omnichannel customer service platform** that works via:

- ✅ Web and mobile voice communication
- ✅ WhatsApp and web-based messaging
- ✅ 24/7 availability for uninterrupted customer support

We integrate with enterprise systems to provide real-time, personalized assistance and accurate responses.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

- **Multi-language Support**  
  Communicate with customers in their preferred language.

- **Pay-as-you-go Model**  
  Scale your operations cost-effectively.

- **Smart Routing Engine**  
  Automatically direct conversations to the most suitable agents or AI assistants.

- **Hybrid AI + Human Agents**  
  Combine the power of automation with the flexibility of human support.

- **Real-time Data Integration**  
  Fetch live data from your systems for contextual conversations.

- **Conversation Summaries & Feedback**  
  Access auto-generated call summaries and gather instant feedback.
